Discover Coxs Bazar Journey Hidden Thinking

Cox's Bazar, located in Bangladesh, is renowned for its stunning sea beach, which stretches over 120 kilometers, making it the longest natural sea beach in the world. This picturesque destination attracts tourists with its golden sands, gentle waves, and vibrant marine life. Visitors can enjoy activities such as surfing, sunbathing, and exploring nearby attractions like Himchari National Park and the enchanting St. Martin's Island.
